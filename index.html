Crystal Collector!

How to Play:

You will be given a random number at the start of the game

There are four crystals below. By clickign on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to you total score.

You win the game by matching you total score to random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
